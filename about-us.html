About NEJM Healer

NEJM Healer is the brand name for a product line that helps teach and assess clinical reasoning.

Created for medical and physician assistant students as well as residents, NEJM Healer was designed to help them efficiently master the clinical reasoning processes necessary to develop their diagnostic and management skills.

Using powerful knowledge and assessment tools, NEJM Healer demystifies, develops, and measures multiple critical clinical reasoning skills, preparing the learner for the next stage of their career.

Origin Story

Even after completing two years of medical school and achieving exceptional grades on numerous exams, I felt clueless when I encountered my very first patient. He presented with a chronic cough.

After 45 minutes of gathering history and performing a physical exam, I was still unable to provide the patient with the differential diagnosis, let alone a care plan.

I stepped out of the patient room to discuss the case with my attending who was immediately able to synthesize the case.

I was impressed by her expertise yet remained perplexed as to how I could have been such a successful student, yet unable to perform even the simplest of clinical tasks.

At the end of my internal medicine residency, I sat for the USMLE Step 3. The exam included a clinical case simulator. I was immediately fascinated.

To this day, I remember my sense of awe as I interacted with this application, engrossed in the process of gathering data to formulate a differential diagnosis.

I left that exam room fully confident that the future of medical assessment must include clinical simulation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many patient cases are available in NEJM Healer?

By practicing with NEJM Healer cases, early student learners gain confidence in the iterative process of gathering patient information, making an initially broad differential diagnosis, and looping back to acquire more pertinent data in order to narrow diagnostic uncertainty sufficiently to make initial decisions on management and treatment. NEJM Healer provides programs with a standardized approach to teaching the diagnostic process.
