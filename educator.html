NEJM Healer for Educators

NEJM Healer is the most comprehensive and engaging way to teach and assess clinical reasoning as well as empower students to understand how clinical experts think.

  • Medical Schools
  • Graduate Training Programs
  • Physician Assistant Programs

Suddenly there's an assessment gap and nowhere along the path from undergraduate education to residency is there a formal assessment of clinical reasoning.

Although most programs offer clinical reasoning, there is little standardization making both medical schools, clinical programs, and the hand-off between the two challenging.

NEJM Healer offers educators a low-cost, low-risk way to enhance the opportunity for learners to increase deliberate practice, regardless of what stage of their learning process they are in.
